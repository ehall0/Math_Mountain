Answer simple math problems correctly to race to the top of the mountain! Answer fast in order to get to the top before your opponent!

RULES:

1. For every question answered correctly the player will advance up the mountain.
--------
After every move, your opponent will automatically take their turn. To your advantage, your opponent only has a 75% chance of answering correctly.

2. After 3 questions correct you will hit a checkpoint.

3. There are 4 checkpoints, checkpoint 0:start , checkpoint 3: finish/top of mountain.

4. First player to reach the third checkpoint wins.
